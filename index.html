Web -> Kindle

Send the webpages from your browser to your kindle in one click!

  1. Drag the bookmark present below to your bookmarks toolbar.
    SendToKindle!
  2. Once you are on a page you want to send to your kindle, click on the 'SendToKindle bookmarklet on your toolbar!
  3. Type your kindle email address when prompted and hit enter!
Note: For this to work, you should add your gmail / googlemail id to your kindle email whitelist, this service uses your gmail account to send the html page via email to the Kindle Personal Documents Service.
